Our story

A little piece of Argentina, without pretending to be fancy.

A quincho is where people gather around the fire. Family, friends, stories, wine, meat, bread, sauce, and the kind of meal that makes everyone slow down for a bit.

At El Quincho, we bring that spirit to Christchurch through wood-fire cooking, honest ingredients, and food made from scratch. It is not complicated. It just takes care, time, and a few good embers.
